Home Photo Shoots

The most popular session is for myself to visit your home - where your pet is most comfortable and feels safe.
1 Hour Shoot: £30
2 Hour Shoot (for muliple animals): £50
After the session, please allow a few days for the photographs to be edited. A 1 hour shoot normally results in 10 to 15 photographs delivered on a memory stick or CD.

Outdoor Shoots

The price for sessions of this style vary depending on time and place.
The standard timed shoot charges apply, but please allow a minimum of £5 on top for travel expenses and outdoor terrain equipment.
These photographs are often popular with dog owners and is the perfect package for capturing your dogs free spirit in an outdoor environment.
I am very happy to come with you on an everyday walk to capture very natural photographs.
Please contact me for a more accurate price quote.

Printing/Framing

All work  is provided on a memory stick or CD ready for printing straight away at your own leisure, however, options are available for myself to print high quality photographs.
Prices depend on photo size and optional frames available.
